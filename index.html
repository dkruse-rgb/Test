Why this tiny arcade rules

It went from test page to actual mini arcade, which is honestly a pretty great glow-up.

Shared sound controls

Mute sound in one game and the others remember it too. Less chaos, more control.

Desktop and mobile ready

Keyboard, swipe, drag, tap, and on-screen buttons depending on the game.

Local score memory

Your browser keeps your best runs and best times so there is always a rematch waiting.

Arcade hub page
