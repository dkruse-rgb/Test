Why this version is fun

I gave the game page a few upgrades so it feels more like a tiny arcade cabinet and less like a homework assignment from 2009.

Desktop and mobile controls

Use arrow keys, WASD, swipe gestures, or on-screen direction buttons.

High score tracking

Your best run is saved in the browser so you have something to chase next time.

Speed scaling

The snake gets quicker as you score, so the pressure ramps up in a good way.

How to play

The rules are blessedly simple, which is exactly why Snake can ruin ten minutes of your life without asking permission.

1

Start moving

Open the game and use your keyboard, swipe, or tap the on-screen arrows to steer.

2

Eat the red food

Each food pickup adds a point and makes your snake a little longer.

3

Do not panic badly

Avoid the walls and your own body. Survive longer, and the game speeds up.
