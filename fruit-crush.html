Swap neighboring fruit to make matches of 3 or more. Big cascades pile on extra points. Hit the target before you run out of moves.

How it works

Tap or click one fruit, then tap a neighboring fruit to swap. Only real matches count. Bad swaps bounce right back like they never happened.

Every match scores points. Cascades score more. Beat the target inside the move limit and the next round gets meaner.
